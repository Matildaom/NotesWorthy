Our Story

Here at NotesWorthy, we are a close-knit team of music professionals dedicated to helping artists achieve their goals.

Matilda, a musician herself, started Notesworthy in 2024 after becoming disillusioned by the standard of professional services available to independent musicians. Notesworthy is a company that serves the musician and has the sole goal of providing reliable services of a high standard for a good price.

We know what it's like struggling to bring your creative passions to fruition.

We understand and we can help!

Matilda O'Mahony, Founder of NotesWorthy
